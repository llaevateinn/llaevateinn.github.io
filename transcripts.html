A Note on Grades:

Only in recent years did I begin to recognize and understand my Asperger traits,
which had previously affected my ability to perform well in exams. Learning how
to cope with these challenges has been an ongoing process.


This also explains why I do better in individual research into specific topics than
in exams -- a result of the "specialized interest".


Another turning point came when I discovered a strong interest in geometry --
first through algebraic topology, then through differential geometry. This
interest gave me renewed motivation and clarity, leading to notable progress
in my academic performance, particularly in the final year of my studies.
